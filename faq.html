9 Fruit is a Christian, non-partisan nonprofit organization built to make charitable giving simpler, more transparent, and more aligned with donor intent.

Rather than creating new charitable programs, 9 Fruit helps donors support vetted organizations already serving children and young adults through health and education initiatives.

9 Fruit provides:

  • donor-directed giving flexibility
  • dedicated matching opportunities for eligible gifts
  • transparent stewardship standards
  • simplified giving infrastructure
  • additional accountability through financial oversight and reporting

This allows donors to support trusted organizations with added clarity and impact.

9 Fruit is a registered 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization. Qualifying gifts may be tax-deductible as permitted by law, and donation receipts are issued promptly.

Transparency is supported through:

  • independent bookkeeping oversight
  • regular financial reconciliation
  • annual independent auditing
  • donor-aligned stewardship standards

The name 9 Fruit is inspired by the nine fruits of the Spirit described by the apostle Paul to the Galatians in Galatians 5:22–23:

Love Joy Peace Patience Kindness Goodness Faithfulness Gentleness Self-control

These qualities reflect the spirit of faithful generosity and thoughtful stewardship that guide 9 Fruit's mission.

We believe giving is strongest when it is rooted in love, guided by wisdom, and carried out with integrity.

9 Fruit exists to help generosity bear good fruit through clarity, stewardship, and faithful giving.
