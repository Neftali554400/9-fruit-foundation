About 9 Fruit

9 Fruit was created to make charitable giving simpler, clearer, and more aligned with the people and causes donors care about most.

Rather than creating new charitable programs, 9 Fruit walks alongside established, reputable organizations already serving children and young adults through health and education initiatives.

This allows generosity to move further through trusted organizations already demonstrating meaningful community impact.

The People Behind It

Our Team

A small team united by faith, a love for children, and a commitment to doing things the right way.

Founder

Ian Gibbs

Founder & Executive Director

James spent over a decade working in international development before founding 9 Fruit. Motivated by faith and a growing frustration with how fragmented charitable giving had become, he set out to build something simpler — a model that puts the donor's intent first and lets generosity move further. He believes even small gifts, given faithfully, can change the course of a child's life.

Sarah Mitchell

Director of Partnerships

Sarah brings fifteen years of nonprofit leadership to 9 Fruit, with deep experience in vetting charitable organizations and building trust-based relationships. She oversees all charity partnerships, ensuring that every organization within the 9 Fruit network meets rigorous standards for accountability, transparency, and proven impact.

Marcus Reid

Operations Lead

Marcus handles the systems and processes that keep 9 Fruit running with integrity. From donation tracking to financial reporting, he ensures that every dollar is accounted for and that donors always have a clear picture of where their gifts are going. He is passionate about the intersection of operational excellence and meaningful mission work.

Why 9 Fruit Exists

Giving should feel thoughtful, transparent, and aligned.

Too often, charitable giving can feel fragmented or unclear.

9 Fruit exists to provide a simple and trustworthy path for donors to support vetted charitable organizations while preserving clarity, donor choice, and financial accountability.

Eligible gifts may receive support through 9 Fruit's dedicated matching model, increasing impact while maintaining responsible stewardship.

How Matching Works

Our Model

9 Fruit supports donor-directed giving through a transparent pass-through framework. This means:

Donors support vetted organizations of their choice

Designated gifts are tracked carefully

Eligible gifts may receive matching support

All giving remains aligned with donor intent and charitable mission
