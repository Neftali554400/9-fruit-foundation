How Matching Works

How Matching Works

9 Fruit's matching model is designed to encourage generosity while increasing impact responsibly.

Eligible gifts may receive a 9% match through a separately maintained matching pool funded specifically for this purpose.

Simple Process

Three steps to greater impact

1

Choose

Select a vetted charitable organization aligned with the causes you care about.

2

Give

Make your gift securely through 9 Fruit's giving platform. Eligible gifts may receive matching support, subject to available matching funds and program guidelines.

3

Deliver

Funds are distributed transparently and carefully in alignment with donor designation and nonprofit standards.
