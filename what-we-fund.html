Stewardship

Stewardship Commitment

9 Fruit maintains clear separation between donor-designated gifts, matching support, and operational resources.

100% of donor-designated gifts are distributed in alignment with donor intent.

100% of eligible matching funds are distributed exclusively for their designated matching purpose, subject to program guidelines and availability.

Operational and administrative support is maintained separately. A limited portion of independently raised administrative resources may periodically support carefully reviewed charitable initiatives aligned with 9 Fruit's broader mission.

Disclosure

Partnership Disclosure

Organizations identified as "9 Fruit Partners" are independently selected charitable organizations whose missions align with 9 Fruit's charitable purpose.

Unless expressly stated otherwise, inclusion does not indicate formal contractual partnership, endorsement, sponsorship, agency relationship, or official affiliation with 9 Fruit.

9 Fruit independently raises awareness and charitable support for these organizations to help strengthen work they are already faithfully carrying out in their communities.
